The Smart Rural Libraries

Rural libraries play very dominant role in the welfare of the rural masses. Rural library can act as community information Centre to improve living condition and quality of life by fulfilling each and every information need, those which are assist individual for their day to day problem solving.
Rural libraries play a key role in the welfare of the rural masses. They can act as a community information center to improve living conditions and quality of life. They can also help individuals solve their day-to-day problems.
Rural libraries provide a variety of free collections to suit the information needs of the community. They also offer educational facilities and basic aims to help with literacy, formal education, and information provision.
